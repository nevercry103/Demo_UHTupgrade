WHY WE HAVE THIS PROJECT
01
⏱️
HARDWARE LIFECYCLE
System Age & End-of-Life Hardware
The UHT-TA automation system has been in operation for nearly 16 years. The S7-300 PLC hardware and associated I/O modules are now out of date, with spare parts increasingly scarce and no longer manufactured. Continued operation poses a significant risk of unplanned downtime.
RISK: HARDWARE OBSOLESCENCE
02
💾
SOFTWARE OBSOLESCENCE
Step7 Manager — Limited Diagnostics & Innovation
The legacy Step7 Manager programming environment is no longer actively supported. It offers limited diagnostics tools, making fault-finding slow and complex. It cannot support modern programming structures, making it difficult to adapt the system when new production demands arise.
RISK: SLOW TROUBLESHOOTING
03
🏢
GROUP COMPLIANCE
BEL Groupe New OT Standard for Siemens at ST3
BEL Groupe has issued a new OT (Operational Technology) standard mandating the adoption of S7-1500 and TIA Portal V19 across all sites including ST3 Vietnam. This upgrade ensures full compliance with group-wide cybersecurity, engineering, and maintenance policies.
DRIVER: GROUP OT STANDARD
04
🌐
DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION
Industrial LAN, Edge Platform & PI Vision
With the S7-1500 and Profinet architecture, the UHT-TA system can be integrated into the Industrial LAN. This enables real-time data collection to the Edge Platform and visualisation through PI Vision — unlocking KPI dashboards, predictive maintenance, and data-driven production decisions.
BENEFIT: INDUSTRY 4.0 READY
